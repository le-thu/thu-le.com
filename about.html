About

I'm based in Saigon and have spent the last four years leading product design at Anduin, a startup creating products and services for fund managers, law firms, and investors in the venture capital and private equity space.

Before Anduin, I designed e-commerce experiences for clients worldwide across various industries as the sole designer at SAI Digital. Prior to that, I was a designer at the cryptocurrency exchange Liquid.

My approach to solving problems is rooted in first principles, systems thinking, and bias for action — aiming to strike the right balance between user needs and business goals. What I get to do with the people I work with is immensely rewarding and satisfying, and I realize I'm incredibly fortunate to be able to say this.
