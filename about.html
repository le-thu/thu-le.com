About

I was born and raised in Saigon, now known as Ho Chi Minh City. When I turned 14, my family and I moved across the globe to St. Petersburg, Florida, where I spent the next decade immersed in a new language and culture. I eventually returned to the bustling streets of Saigon, the place I feel most at home.

My interest in building things stems from countless hours spent with LEGO sets as a child. I'd credit my passion for design to the time I spent dabbling in photography and filmmaking during high school.

For the past four years, I've led product design at Anduin, a startup based in Vietnam and the US creating products and services for fund managers, law firms, and investors in the venture capital and private equity space. Before Anduin, I designed e-commerce experiences for clients worldwide across various industries at SAI Digital. Prior to that, I was a designer at the cryptocurrency exchange Liquid (acquired by bitFlyer).

My approach to solving problems is rooted in first principles, systems thinking, and bias for action — aiming to strike the right balance between user needs and business goals. What I get to do with the people I work with is immensely rewarding and satisfying, and I feel incredibly fortunate to be able to say this.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books and catching up on blogs. I also like journaling to clear my thoughts, documenting interesting things I learn, and slowly building this site into a proper digital garden. Read about what I've been up to, view links I bookmarked, and check out my /uses page.

I'm open to chats and coffee if you're in town. Say hello!

Past life

After graduating with a Bachelor's in Biology and realizing that I didn't want to go to pharmacy school, I somehow got my first full-time job right after college at a mom-and-pop print shop thanks to the fact that I had taught myself Photoshop and Illustrator, and designed band posters for my grandpa back in school, giving me some design experience.

Photography was a big part of my life, sadly I don't have time for it these days. My photos are on Flickr (although I’ve since lost access to my account) and Instagram. Making vlogs was another hobby that gave me a lot of joy.
