Things worth spending on

Nov 9, 2024

Worthwhile investments, in my experience, and occasional treats that make my day more delightful:

  • Iced latte or cold brew: brings joy to my morning.
  • Travel: whether it's an overseas trip or a nearby adventure, it expands my mind and makes me realize we're tiny specks in this universe.
  • Dining out: trying new cuisines and enjoying good food, and quality time with people I care about.
  • Good experiences with friends and family: investing in my relationships and creating shared memories.
  • A comfortable pair of noise-canceling headphones: helps me focus during work or running, and is a godsend on long flights.
  • A dedicated workspace: a proper space for focused work on days I work from home, as well as for learning and researching.
  • A high-quality physical notebook: for sketching and thinking out loud, and makes me want to sketch and write.
  • Books: open my eyes, change my mind, and also remind me that we're tiny specks in this universe.
  • iPad mini: helps me read and enjoy more books than ever this year.
  • Gym membership: or any physical activities, for my physical and mental well-being.
  • A good pair of running shoes: as someone who never owned a pair before and recently got into running, they make a world of difference.
  • Cleaning and laundry services: optimize my routine and free up my time for more important things.
  • Donations and tips: a way to give back and show my appreciation.
  • YouTube Premium: no more skipping through ads and noise, ultimately a huge time saver for me.
  • My personal domain: allows me to build an online presence.
  • Well-designed apps and products that nudge me to improve my habits and systems, or simply add joy to my life and make me feel good.
